# Working Alongside David Mecey

This section preserves one of the most meaningful chapters of Tim's
career.

Working alongside my friend and internationally recognized Playboy
photographer David Mecey has been one of the highlights of this chapter
of my career. As both a mentor and a friend, David has had a meaningful
influence on my creative journey. Having the opportunity to contribute
to MECÉ Magazine---and to photograph the cover and centerfold model for
three of the last four issues---has been an honor that I deeply
appreciate.
