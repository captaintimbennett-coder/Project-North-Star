# Sprint 00 — Project Foundation

Status: Complete

## Goal

Establish the governing vision, product principles, technical direction, and
long-term standards for Project North Star.

## Scope

- Define Tim Bennett’s personal and professional context.
- Define Project North Star’s mission and pillars.
- Establish the platform as a digital headquarters rather than a portfolio.
- Identify the core brands and future capabilities.
- Select the preferred technical stack.
- Record how Codex should act as architect, designer, and development partner.

## Deliverables

- Master vision and brand direction
- Initial site information architecture
- Technical philosophy and preferred stack
- Long-term feature inventory
- Development and AI collaboration standards
- Definition of success

## Decisions Made

- Project North Star will support Tim Bennett Photography, Lone Star Retreat,
  personal journal content, education, and future ventures.
- The brand position is the intersection of precision and imagination.
- The preferred stack is Next.js, React, TypeScript, Supabase, Vercel, Stripe,
  and GitHub.
- Ownership, maintainability, performance, security, and scalability take
  priority over restrictive short-term convenience.
- Safety, respect, and professionalism are foundational values for Lone Star
  Retreat.

## Deferred Items

- Final public copy
- Final photography selection and licensing metadata
- Production domain and deployment
- Database schema implementation
- Authentication, payments, applications, and integrations

## Dependencies

- Tim’s approval of the mission and brand direction
- A development workspace capable of supporting the preferred stack
- Continued documentation of material product decisions

## Acceptance Criteria

- [x] The platform mission is documented.
- [x] The primary brands and audiences are identified.
- [x] The visual and emotional direction is defined.
- [x] The preferred technical stack is recorded.
- [x] Future capabilities are acknowledged without premature implementation.
- [x] Codex’s role and decision-making expectations are clear.

## Notes for Future Codex Sessions

Treat the master vision as the governing product brief. Challenge work that
turns the platform into a generic photography template, creates avoidable
technical debt, or weakens the premium, professional, and expandable direction.
