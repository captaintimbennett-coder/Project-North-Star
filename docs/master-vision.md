# Project North Star Master Vision

## Mission

This platform is the digital home of Tim Bennett and the foundation for
everything he will build over the next decade. It is not merely a photography
website.

Tim Bennett is the primary public-facing brand: the photographer, educator,
mentor, creator, and person visitors choose to work with. Project North Star is
the philosophy guiding every experience he creates. It represents direction,
purpose, excellence, craftsmanship, and legacy.

The platform should communicate excellence, discipline, artistry,
professionalism, trust, warmth, and purpose. Every design and architecture
decision should support those values.

## Brand idea

The brand hierarchy is:

1. **Tim Bennett** — Photographer, Educator, Creator, Mentor
2. **Project North Star** — Tim’s philosophy, standard, and North Star
3. **Experiences** — Private client boudoir and glamour, Lone Star Retreat,
   workshops and education, one-on-one mentoring, editorial and publications
4. **Shared platform services** — CRM, client portal, scheduling, contracts,
   payments, galleries, email, analytics, and automation

Project North Star should unify these experiences without replacing the
individual behind them.

Tim’s most distinctive position is the intersection of two worlds:

- aviation: discipline, systems, precision, preparation, leadership
- photography: creativity, artistry, storytelling, confidence, connection

The brand expression is where precision and imagination intersect.

## Platform scope

The platform is the digital home for:

- Tim Bennett Photography
- Lone Star Retreat
- Project North Star
- future education and mentoring
- premium client experiences
- future creative and business ventures

It should feel like the digital headquarters of a premium creative brand—not a
traditional portfolio template.

## Experience principles

- Luxury without flashiness
- Editorial rather than corporate
- Confident without arrogance
- Warm without becoming overly feminine or masculine
- Minimal, cinematic, and intentional
- Photography-led, with generous breathing room
- Mobile-first, accessible, and fast

The emotional reference is entering a thoughtful luxury boutique hotel.

## Visual direction

- Black, charcoal, bronze, warm gold, muted champagne, ivory, and warm white
- Elegant serif display typography paired with a restrained modern sans serif
- Large cinematic imagery
- Magazine-inspired hierarchy and composition
- Subtle animation that never competes with performance

Avoid bright colors, neon accents, generic photography clichés, cheap luxury,
trend chasing, clutter, and corporate stiffness.

## Long-term capabilities

The architecture should eventually support:

- curated portfolio collections
- client galleries and private portals
- model and photographer applications
- Retreat registration
- payments
- educational products and digital downloads
- Project North Star journal publishing
- newsletter and CRM integrations
- scheduling
- media management
- secure administrative tools
- future mobile application support

## Definition of success

Visitors should first understand who Tim Bennett is, then understand what he
believes, discover the experiences he has created, and choose how to work with
him. They should recognize that Tim operates at a high level and is building
something larger than photography alone. The platform should evolve for years
without requiring a complete redesign.
